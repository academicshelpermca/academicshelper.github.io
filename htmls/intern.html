Important topics for Technical Round

Following topics are very important to crack any coding round. They are the most frequently asked and must to do topics. You should go through them.

  • Arrays
  • Strings
  • Sorting
  • Searching
  • Linked Lists
  • Stacks & Queues
  • Trees & BST
  • STLs (Vector, Map...etc)
  • Heaps
  • Recursion
  • Hashing
  • Graphs
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • Dynamic Programming(DP)
  • Divide & Conquer
  • Backtracking
  • Bit Magic
  • Advanced Data Structures

Project Discussion

If you are applying for an internship, there must be at least one project listed in your resume and for placement, there should be atleast 2-3 projects. You have to prepare each and every thing that you have used in your project. Following questions are asked related to your projects.

  1. Technologies that you have used in project(s)
  2. Why you use these technologies?
  3. SQL queries, tables & databases
  4. Difficulties that you have faced in your project
  5. How did you overcome those difficulties?

Frequently Asked Question in Hr Round

  1. Tell me about yourself
  2. What are your strength and weakness?
  3. What are your goals ?
  4. Who has inspired you the most in your life and why ?
  5. Explain the difference between group and team. Are you a team player?
  6. Can you describe your time management skills?
  7. Have you ever worked under pressure ?
  8. How quickly do you adapt to new technology?
  9. What do you know about our company ?
  10. What are your expectations from us ?
  11. Why do you want to join our company ?
  12. Why should we hire you ?
  13. Where do you see yourself in 5 years ?
